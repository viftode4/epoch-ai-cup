# FIGURE SPECS — AI Cup Congress Pitch

_Last updated: 2026-04-11_

## Global figure rules
- max 1 dominant figure per slide
- max 2 annotation clusters per slide
- max 1 sketch/hash motif family per slide
- use orange for urgency/action, teal for validation/trust
- use notebook marks to clarify, not decorate

---

## Slide 1 — Opening
### Dominant figure
Shared-airspace composition.

### Elements
- 2–3 trajectory traces crossing the upper-right visual field
- 1–2 turbine silhouettes near lower-right
- one overlap label: `airspace overlap`
- one note: `real-world sensing problem`

### Purpose
Show tension and relevance instantly.
