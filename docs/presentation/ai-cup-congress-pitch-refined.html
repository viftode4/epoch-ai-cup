05 / 07
three lessons changed the project

The gains came from changing our framing.

The strongest improvements were conceptual before they were numerical.

Insight 01

Optimize the ranking

Macro-mAP rewards ordering, so the model had to match the metric rather than generic classification loss.

Insight 02

Treat shift as real behavior

Month shift reflected migration and operating variation, not just dataset noise.

Insight 03

Keep ecology inside the model

Movement, radar, and context only helped when they stayed tied to mitigation meaning.

generalization > local gain
This is where the workflow became insight, and the insight became architecture.
